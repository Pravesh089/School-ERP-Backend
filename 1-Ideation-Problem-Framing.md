# Phase 1: Ideation & Problem Framing

## 1. Problem Statement

> **Schools rely on physical registers and notebooks to manage academic, administrative, and student-related data, leading to inefficiency, data inconsistency, difficulty in retrieval, lack of analytics, and high operational overhead.**

The goal is to build a **web-based School Management System** that digitizes these records with **role-based access**, **auditability**, and **simple reporting**, without unnecessary complexity.
